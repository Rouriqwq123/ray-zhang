About Me

Hi, I'm Ray Zhang, a first-year Computer Engineering student at the University of Waterloo.

I'm interested in artificial intelligence, software, and the way technology can shape how people create, communicate, and solve problems. I enjoy building things, but I also care a lot about understanding how they work beneath the surface.

Outside of engineering, music is a big part of who I am. I compose, share content on YouTube and Bilibili, and co-founded RS Studio with close friends. Working in both music and tech has shaped the way I think — creativity matters in both, just in different ways.

I'm especially drawn to projects that combine technical skill with creative thinking, and that's the direction I want to keep growing in.
